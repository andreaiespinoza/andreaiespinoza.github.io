Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2024

Privacy Commitment

Selah is built with privacy as a core value. The app does not track you, does not collect personal data, and does not require an account. All information remains on your device and is never shared or transmitted to external servers except when retrieving Scripture or prayer content.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Selah handles information within the iOS application. Selah helps you pause for spiritual reflection—reading Scripture, praying, or meditating—before accessing selected apps.

2. Information Selah Does Not Collect

Selah does not collect, store, transmit, or sell:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, UUID, advertising IDs)
  • Browsing history or networks visited
  • Contact lists
  • Keystrokes
  • App usage or time spent within other apps
  • Any data for advertising or marketing

The app does not use third-party analytics of any kind.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and never leaves the iOS sandbox:

3.1 User Preferences

  • Selected Bible translation (KJV, WEB, ASV, or RVR1960)
  • Pause duration (15–60 seconds)
  • Auto-relock interval (15 minutes to 4 hours)
  • Which apps/categories are selected for blocking

3.2 User-Created Content

  • Custom prayers
  • Custom or saved Bible verses

3.3 Usage Statistics

  • Total prayer/reflection time
  • Daily streak count
  • Longest streak
  • Number of unlocks per day

3.4 Screen Time Settings

Managed through Apple's FamilyControls framework. Selah cannot see which apps you open or how long you spend in them; it only knows which apps you manually selected for blocking.

All data is stored securely on your device and may be included in iCloud/iTunes backups based on your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Selah retrieves Scripture and prayer content from trusted third-party sources. No personal data is shared with those services.

4.1 Bible APIs

Services used:

  • bible-api.com
  • bible.helloao.org

Purpose: Retrieve Bible verses by reference and translation.

Data sent: Only the verse reference (e.g., "John 3:16") and translation code.

Data received: Verse text.

No personal information is transmitted.

4.2 Joshua Project API

Selah uses publicly available information provided through the Joshua Project API to display daily unreached people group data.

Data sent: Developer API key (not linked to the user).

Data received: People group name, region, language, population, and prayer points.

Caching: Data is stored until 2:00 AM CST to minimize requests.

These services may log your device's IP address, which is standard for all web requests.

5. Permissions Required

5.1 Screen Time Access (FamilyControls)

Used to block apps you select.

This permission cannot read your app activity, usage time, or browsing history.

No other permissions are requested.

6. Data Security

  • All data stays within Apple's secure app sandbox.
  • Selah does not transmit, sync, or upload your data.
  • When you delete Selah, all locally stored app data is permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

Selah collects no personal information, making it safe for all ages. Parental supervision is recommended for younger users due to the nature of spiritual content.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of any changes.

9. Open Source Transparency

Parts of Selah's codebase are available on GitHub for transparency. Users may review how data is handled and verify privacy claims.

10. Your Rights

Since Selah stores no personal data on external servers:

  • Access: All data is visible inside the app.
  • Deletion: Delete data within the app or uninstall Selah.
  • Portability: Device backups (iCloud/iTunes) may contain your Selah data locally.
